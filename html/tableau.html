Profitability Analysis

California has been the most profitable state. We will take a closer look to examine what is driving the profits in California.



California experiences record high profits in November 2013, one month after experiencing a record low in October 2013. November 2014 profits were the second highest California has experienced and we're hoping to experience a new record high in the near future.



In California, office supplies are the most profitable, technology is the second most profitable, and furniture is the least profitable. We hope to improve furniture profitability by increasing furniture marketing efforts.



In July 2011 office supplies sales in California reached a record high. Since then sales have decreased about 50% but we are hoping to steadily increase office supplies sales over the next year.
